Privacy Policy


Effective Date: January 26, 2026

Privacy is simple with Piano Teacher.
This is a static web application and it prioritizes your data security by simply not collecting any at all.


1. No Data Collection

Piano Teacher does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.
We do not have accounts, we do not track your location, and we do not use cookies.


2. Local Storage Only

Any settings you make within the app are saved locally on your device using your browser's internal storage.
This data never leaves your computer and is deleted if you clear your browser data or uninstall the app.


3. No Third Parties

We do not share data with third parties because we have no data to share.
There are no advertisements or hidden analytics scripts in this application.


4. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this policy, please reach out to callumoprey@gmail.com.
